# Gratuity Calculator UAE - Admin Dashboard Guide

Welcome to your command center. This guide covers how to use the advanced features implemented in your admin dashboard.

## 1. Content Management

### Managing Pages
- Navigate to **Content > Pages** to see all static landing pages.
- Use the **Search bar** to find specific pages by title or slug.
- The **SEO Score** column indicates how well-optimized each page is based on meta tags and content quality.

### Writing Blog Posts
- Navigate to **Content > Blogs**.
- Click **New Post** to open the editor.
- Use the **Rich Text Editor** to format your content. Features include headers, links, and lists.

## 3. Smart Linker

Internal linking is critical for SEO authority flow. 
- In the **Settings** tab of the editor, use the **Smart Linker**.
- Click **Scan for Internal Links**.
- The system will find existing pages on your site that match keywords in your current content.
- Click **Copy Link** to get the HTML code to paste into your article.

## 4. Asset Studio (Media)

Manage all your imagery in **Media > Media Library**.
- Upload WebP images for the best performance.
- Use the **Asset Preview** to verify imagery before inserting link URLs into your pages.

## 5. SEO Command Center

Technical health is monitored in real-time.
- Check the **SEO Dashboard** for overall organic health.
